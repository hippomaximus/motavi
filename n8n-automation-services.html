What an n8n automation agency does

n8n is an open-source workflow automation platform — the engine that connects your apps, APIs, and databases and runs multi-step processes for you, around the clock. An n8n agency turns that capability into working systems: identifying which of your manual processes are worth automating, designing the logic, building the workflows with proper error handling, and keeping them running as your tools and APIs change.

The reason teams hire a specialist rather than learning it in-house is the gap between a proof-of-concept and a production system. A workflow that works once in a demo is easy. A workflow that handles real volume, retries when an API hiccups, routes errors to a human, and doesn't silently break at 3am is engineering. That's what you're paying for.

Why self-hosted n8n, and why us

We deploy n8n on your own infrastructure rather than a hosted automation cloud. That eliminates per-task pricing entirely — the meter that makes Zapier and Make expensive at scale — and keeps your customer data on servers you control, which matters for anyone handling records under privacy regulation.

Most agencies build with other people's money on other people's stacks. We run eight live businesses — e-commerce, importing, publishing, SaaS — on self-hosted n8n with a Postgres backbone and Telegram alerting. Every workflow we sell, we've already shipped to production for ourselves.
