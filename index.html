The real cost

Here's what's broken — and why the common fix doesn't work.

Most teams reach for Zapier or Make, then hit a wall: per-task pricing that balloons at scale, fragile connections that break silently, and no real control over their own data.

The Zapier bill keeps climbing

Per-task pricing punishes you for growing. At volume, a workflow that costs $20 a month on n8n runs $800+ on Zapier. You're renting your own automation.

Workflows break at 3am

No retry logic, no error routing, no monitoring. An API changes, a connection drops, and nobody knows until a customer complains the next morning.

Your data lives on someone else's server

For anyone handling customer records, payments, or anything regulated, sending it through a third-party cloud is a liability you didn't sign up for.
